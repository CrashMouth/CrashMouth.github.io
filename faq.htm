- Technical Aspects -

What's inside my mouthguard?

  • How does the mouthguard work?

Each CrashMouth mouthguard comes with bluetooth software that displays all diagnostics on our companion app, letting coaches know all of our sensors' information in real-time. As for the sensors themselves, we have three. Our concussion sensor, located in the back of the mouthguard, determines whether or not a blow to the head surpasses a predetermined force constant (930N). If this constant is reached, the coach is informed that the player may be in danger of recieving a concussion. Our dehydration sensor, located in the front of the mouthguard, picks up on saliva content and gives readings on electrolyte levels and the like. And, lastly, our heart rate sensor is located near the gums so that coaches are able to pick up on abnormal heart rate fluctuations.

  • How long does the charge of my CrashMouth mouthguard last?

CrashMouth mouthguards will have a 6hr battery life when connected via Bluetooth to the CrashMouth app.
